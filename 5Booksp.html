Top 5 Books That Changed My Life

February 8, 2024

Books have the power to change our perspective. Here are five books that deeply impacted me:

  • The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho — A journey of personal legend.
  • Atomic Habits by James Clear — Small changes, big results.
  • Man’s Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl — Finding meaning in suffering.
  • Start With Why by Simon Sinek — Understanding purpose and leadership.
  • Into the Wild by Jon Krakauer — A story of adventure and freedom.
