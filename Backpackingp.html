What I Learned Backpacking Alone

April 15, 2024

Backpacking alone was one of the most transformative experiences of my life. I learned resilience, how to enjoy my own company, and how to embrace uncertainty.

At first, the solitude was unsettling. I remember standing in a crowded train station in Prague, not understanding a word of the announcements, and feeling utterly alone. But in that moment, I realized no one was coming to save me—I had to rely on myself. That realization lit a fire in me.

If I wanted to hike all day and eat street food by a river at sunset, I did. If I wanted to sleep in or read for hours in a café, no one questioned it. I had complete autonomy.
